## 📊 Survey Analysis Guide

### SUS Score Calculation Method
- Odd-numbered items (Q5, Q7, Q9, Q11, Q13): Score - 1
- Even-numbered items (Q6, Q8, Q10, Q12, Q14): 5 - Score
- Total Score × 2.5 = SUS Score (0-100 points)
- 68 points or above = Acceptable usability

### Statistical Analysis Items
1. **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, standard deviation, median
2. **Correlation Analysis**: Correlation between experience and satisfaction, experience and accuracy evaluation
3. **Comparative Analysis**: Feature-specific satisfaction comparison
4. **Qualitative Analysis**: Coding and thematic analysis of free-text responses
